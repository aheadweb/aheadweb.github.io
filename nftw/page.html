Squid Game #Players NFT

About collection

Squid Game #Player NFTs is a unique Play-To-Earn NFT collection built on the Binance Smart Chain network, that consists of 10,000 #Players that can be used in 6 different GameFi Metaverse games. One mint of NFT #Player costs 0,6 BNB.

Team of designers portrayed most of the series' main and supporting characters. The uniqueness of the collection is in its style and approach to design.
Six Metaverse games will repeat the main rounds from the series, where players will also fight and compete for the grand prize of $150,000.
