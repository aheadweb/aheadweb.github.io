Guna

About collection

A collection of 10,000 unique NFTs, each featuring a dream girl in the guise of various heroines from the most popular multiverses. Only in this collection you will be able to see your favorite characters with various accessories in a variety of looks, from Cruella to Catwoman, as well as many other skins from Anime and the most popular cinematic universes.

Concept

The concept of the Guna collection is that in one collection you can see many heroines from your favorite films, series or cartoons. For each character, a lot of unique traits were created, with the help of which the images turned out to be even more unique and memorable. Where else will you see Scarlet Witch with a tattoo on her shoulder or Mulan dressed as Snow White? All this, and much more, was displayed on the unique beauty of a girl with bright looks that will perfectly complement any NFT collection.
