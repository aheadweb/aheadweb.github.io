Squid Game #Players NFT

About collection

Squid Game #Player NFTs is a unique Play-To-Earn NFT collection built on the BNB Chain network, that consists of 10,000 #Players that can be used in 6 different GameFi Metaverse games. One mint of NFT #Player costs 0,6 BNB.
Team of designers portrayed most of the series' main and supporting characters. The uniqueness of the collection is in its style and approach to design. Six Metaverse games will repeat the main rounds from the series, where players will also fight and compete for the grand prize of $150,000.

Concept

Nowadays there is an NFT boom and a lot of new people are learning about it. The authors of the idea of this collection were inspired by the popular TV show “Squid Game”. They decided to implement the same game based on the Play-To-Earn concept for everyone to try their luck and test their skills.
A lot of work has been done to develop the style, concept and variety of the collection. After a long and painstaking work, we demonstrated to the customer a unique NFT collection.
