Injustice towards people
Injustice towards people because of their religion is an acute problem for the entire world community. With the help of a caring community, all stereotypes will be eradicated, and the topic will be globally covered.

How will you help people?
The main goal of the project is to raise the necessary amount of money for charity in order to transfer money to funds for those in need and build a mosque. 50% of the proceeds will go to direct expenses related to helping people, transfer to charitable funds for those in need, part of these funds will be invested in the construction of a mosque.
